About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Robotics at Hanyang University ERICA. My research centers on soft robotics — designing compliant machines and giving them embodied intelligence, underwater navigation, and a fluid-mechanical understanding of their own bodies.

Across these threads I am drawn to systems where mechanical compliance, distributed control, and biological intuition converge — machines that move with the elegance of living things.

03

Research

  1. R/01

    Soft Robotics

    Designing compliant machines with elastic materials, distributed actuation, and bio-inspired morphology.

  2. R/02

    Embodied Intelligence in Soft Robotics

    Exploiting body compliance and morphological computation so soft robots can sense, decide, and adapt with minimal centralized control.

  3. R/03

    Underwater Navigation for Soft Robots

    Localization, mapping, and path planning for compliant underwater platforms whose body shape itself deforms during motion.

  4. R/04

    Fluid Mechanics of Soft Swimmers

    Modeling fluid–structure interaction to optimize jet propulsion, drag, and three-dimensional maneuvering in water.
